Average Question 1

Question: The average of 10, 15, and 20 is:

Answer: The average is 15.

Explanation: The average is calculated as (Sum of numbers) / (Number of numbers). In this case, it's (10 + 15 + 20) / 3 = 15.

Average Question 2

Question: If the average of five numbers is 24, what is their sum?

Answer: The sum is 120.

Explanation: The sum is calculated as (Average) * (Number of numbers). In this case, it's 24 * 5 = 120.

Average Question 6

Question: The average of six consecutive even numbers is 24. What is the smallest of these numbers?

Average Question 7

Question: The average of 8 numbers is 48. If two numbers are removed, the average becomes 52. What are the two numbers?

Answer: The two numbers are 20 and 28.

Explanation: Let the sum of all 8 numbers be X. So, (X/8) = 48. Therefore, X = 48 * 8 = 384. Now, when two numbers are removed, the sum becomes (X - 20 - 28). The new average is (X - 20 - 28)/6 = 52. Solving for X gives X = 384, which confirms that the two numbers are 20 and 28.

Average Question 9

Question: The average of 15 numbers is 18. If the average of the first 10 numbers is 16, what is the average of the last 5 numbers?

Answer: The average of the last 5 numbers is 28.

Explanation: To find the average of the last 5 numbers, we can use the formula for weighted average: (Total Average) = [(Average of the first 10 numbers * Number of first numbers) + (Average of the last 5 numbers * Number of last numbers)] / Total Number of Numbers. Solving for the average of the last 5 numbers, we get (18 * 15 - 16 * 10) / 5 = 28.

Average Question 11

Question: The average age of a group of 6 friends is 28 years. If the age of a new friend is added to the group, the average becomes 30 years. What is the age of the new friend?

Answer: The age of the new friend is 36 years.

Explanation: Let the sum of the ages of the 6 friends be X. So, (X/6) = 28. Therefore, X = 28 * 6 = 168. Now, when the new friend is added, the sum becomes (X + Age of the new friend). The new average is (X + Age of the new friend)/7 = 30. Solving for the age of the new friend, we get (168 + Age of the new friend)/7 = 30, which leads to the age of the new friend being 36 years.
