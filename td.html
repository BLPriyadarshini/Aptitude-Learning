Time and Distance Question 1

Question: A car travels at a speed of 60 miles per hour. How far will it travel in 3 hours?

Answer: The car will travel 180 miles.

Explanation: Distance = Speed × Time. In this case, it's 60 mph × 3 hours = 180 miles.

Time and Distance Question 2

Question: A train travels at a speed of 90 kilometers per hour. How long will it take to travel 270 kilometers?

Answer: It will take 3 hours.

Explanation: Time = Distance / Speed. In this case, it's 270 km / 90 km/h = 3 hours.
